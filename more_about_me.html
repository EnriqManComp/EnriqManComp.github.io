About Me

My interest in programming started back in high school, and since then, I’ve been passionate about programming, technology and problem-solving .

Professional Experience

Over the past few years, I’ve worked as an IT Engineer and Software Engineer, where I specialized in data integration, analytics automation, and infrastructure optimization. I developed data pipelines and interactive dashboards that provided actionable insights into network performance and resource utilization, directly improving operational efficiency and decision-making. I take pride in developing solutions that not only process data efficiently but also empower teams with clear, data-backed insights.

Whether it’s building a robust data architecture, developing predictive models, or visualizing insights that guide strategic choices, I focus on creating solutions that deliver real impact. My goal is to keep learning, experimenting, and contributing to projects where data helps drive smarter, more efficient decisions.
