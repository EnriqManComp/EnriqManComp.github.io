About Me

Hi! My name is Enrique Companioni. I recently earned a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the Center for Scientific Research and Higher Education of Ensenada (CICESE). My interest in programming started back in high school, and since then, I’ve been passionate about technology and problem-solving. This early curiosity led me to pursue a professional path in tech, where I continue to grow my skills and work on projects that make an impact.

I am particularly passionate about designing and implementing end-to-end solutions using big data technologies. I also enjoy developing machine learning models and applying them to real-world scenarios. My projects typically involve integrating data from multiple sources and delivering actionable insights to stakeholders, tailored to their decision-making needs.
